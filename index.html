About Holy Cross

About us

Holy Cross Girls School was  established in the year -2014 in the lush green environment at Shastri Nagar , Madhepura. The school is recognized for its commitment towards excellence. This is an English medium school approved by Government of Bihar.

  • At Holy Cross Girls School, students receive so much more than an ordinary education.
  • We pride ourselves on creating a unique, exciting environment that embraces all types of learners and promotes growth both academically and personally.
  • Students and families are also welcomed into a dynamic and supportive community.
  • Interested in learning more about Holy Cross Girls School? We invite you to keep exploring our website, and get in touch with questions about our values, academics, community and more. Join us today!

Admissions

Holy Cross Girls School is welcoming  interested  applications for coming sessions, admission start from Nursery to Class- VIII . It is  subject to Vacancies , Admission is granted on academic grounds irrespective of caste, creed and community.

  • Students are selected through the results of the admission test that they have to undergo upon registration with the school.
  • Fees are to be paid in "quarterly basis"
  • Admission is based on the performance of the candidate at the written test they are placed in the merit list and admitted as per existing vacancy in a particular class.
  • The candidate is graded as per her performance and suitability for particular standard.
